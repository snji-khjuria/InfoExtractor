    About Pink Pearl Resort and Fun City

    check-in 1 PM | check-out 11 AM

    The Pink Pearl resort offers comfortable accommodation with state of art facilities to its guests. The reception is semi-circula ... More

    The Pink Pearl resort offers comfortable accommodation with state of art facilities to its guests. The reception is semi-circular with a glass wall overlooking the lawn. The lobby is comfortable, decorated with plush sofas neatly arranged so that a feeling of openness automatically comes to you upon entering. It is located conveniently too, en route to the Pink City Jaipur, only 15 kilometres short of the city landmark. The airport is 22 kilometres away and the railway station and bus stand is just 14 kilometres away. The business centre and shopping complex is also very close being only 6 kilometres away.

    Hotel Facilities The Conference Hall and business centre is located in the ground floor and has a capacity to accommodate 90 people.

    Room Amenities The hotel offers Deluxe rooms, Superior rooms and Suites. The Deluxe rooms are well furnished with double or twin beds, a comfortable sofa and table, table and chair, colour satellite television, telephone, mini bar, good spacious cupboards and an electronic locker. The bathroom is spacious with a shower cubicle, telephone, disposable bag, toilet paper, soap, moisturizer, comb, shower cap, and clean hand and bath towels.

    Dining The ground floor houses the Coffee Shop with sitting facilities both inside and outside in the balcony overlooking the vast expanse of the beautiful lawn. The Gothic Restaurant called Spirit of Past situated on the second floor has been created with the intention of keeping the retro zing alive. If you chance to visit this place, enjoy with the range of Continental and Mughal Indian cuisine.The restaurant offers mini meals and snacks at nominal prices keeping in mind the variety of people who come here. The restaurant also organises packages for bigger lots like school children and college youths and this can vary from simple snacks to heavy vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals. Visitors can also drink in the bar of the hotel

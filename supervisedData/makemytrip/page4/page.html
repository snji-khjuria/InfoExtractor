    About Crystal Sarovar Premiere

    check-in 2 PM | check-out 12 PM

    Location: Just 3 km from the iconic Taj Mahal, Crystal Sarovar Premiere is situated less than 5 km from Agra Fort and Jama Masji ... More

    Location: Just 3 km from the iconic Taj Mahal, Crystal Sarovar Premiere is situated less than 5 km from Agra Fort and Jama Masjid. It is a short drive from Agra City Railway Station (7 km) which makes it easy for the guest to commute.

    Room Amenities: There are 136 air-conditioned rooms that include amenities such as work desk, flat screen television, premium bath amenities. Select rooms (Premium and Studio) offer stunning views of Taj Mahal.

    Hotel Facilities: The hotel offers facilities like front desk, Wi-Fi, laundry, doctor on call, railway station transfers and many more. Fitness freaks can work out at the in-house fitness centre or swim a few laps in the pool. There is a special kids activity zone, in-house spa, banquet hall and a business centre.

    Dining: For dining, the hotel offers a 24-hour, multi-cuisine restaurant (Lattice), poolside dining for barbeque and buffet spreads (Sky Grill), and drinks and snacks at Fizz.

    Pay at Checkout

    Pay at Checkout policy is valid for hotels in India only.

    After booking a hotel online on Makemytrip, the hotel may ask the guest to make partial prepayment a few days before the checkin to guarantee the booking, per hotel's T&C.

    The remaining amount needs to be paid by the guest at the hotel. Guests might be asked to pay in cash if the credit card machine at the hotel is absent or dysfunctional.

    If the guest plans to check-in at the hotel after 5:00 pm, they must inform the hotel in advance. The hotel can release the room in case you have not checked-in by 5:00 pm and have not informed the hotel about your late check-in.

    Reward Points redemption option is not applicable on bookings made using the "Pay at Checkout" option.

    Some hotels might ask for a security deposit of up to 100% of the booking amount at the time of check-in; denial of paying security deposit, may lead to hotelier not allowing check-in.

    Guest must pay full amount at time of check-out; hotel's payment cannot be withheld by customer in any case after completing stay.

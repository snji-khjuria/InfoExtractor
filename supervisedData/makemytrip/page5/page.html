    About Hotel Royal Orchid Jaipur

    check-in 2 PM | check-out 12 PM

    Enjoy a refreshing dip in the rooftop pool at the Royal Orchid Jaipur, which features multiple restaurants & a lounge. Lo ... More

    Enjoy a refreshing dip in the rooftop pool at the Royal Orchid Jaipur, which features multiple restaurants & a lounge.

    Location: Hotel Royal Orchid is situated on Tonk Road, which is the business hub of the Pink City, Jaipur. It is 3 km away from the Jaipur International Airport and 10 km away from the Jaipur Railway Station. It is 6 km away from Birla Temple, while Jantar Mantar, Hawa Mahal and City Palace are all 9 km away.

    Room Amenities: Hotel Royal Orchid, Jaipur comprises of 139 gracefully designed rooms including 9 plunge pool rooms and a plunge pool suite. They are classified as Deluxe Rooms, Club Rooms, Plunge Pool Rooms, Plunge Pool Suites and Suites. These plush rooms come with a tea/coffee maker, a fully stocked mini-bar and a 42-inch LCD TV with satellite channels. The en suite bathroom comes with toiletries and a bathtub. The rooms offer a view of the urban cityscape.

    Hotel Facilities: The beautiful rooftop swimming pool along with the state-of-the-art fitness centre and gym provide an ultimate escape into relaxation. With meeting rooms, conference rooms and banquet rooms, Hotel Royal Orchid has the capacity to hold large and small scale events including destination weddings. With concierge services and currency exchange, the reception team ensures a lovely vacation for all.

    Dining: Ginseng is the hotel's award-winning Oriental restaurant, while Limelight is the all-day restaurant that serves multi-cuisine food. Tiger Trail offers Indian cuisine that captures the true essence of North India in the form of kebabs and grills. To add to the excitement, it is served in a classy fine-dining setting. Refresh with a soothing drink at Salsa Lounge Bar which serves fine beverages alongside some lovely appetizers.
